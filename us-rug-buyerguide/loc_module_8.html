Rug Cleaning Tips
Machine Washing Tip
Remove the rug from the rug pad before washing. Before cleaning, please select the appropriate washing machine based on the rug size shown on the right.
Machine-wash the rug in cold water with the patterned side facing outward. Do not use detergents containing bleach.

Drying Tip
We strongly recommend hanging the rug outside to dry. (The rug dries quickly.) If hanging is not possible,dry on low heat. Do not use fabric softeners.
Vacuum Cleaning Tip
Vacuum on the lowest setting and avoid using industrial vacuums with brushes. Be careful not to vacuum the edges of the rug.

Rug Pad Cleaning Tips
Do not machine-wash the rug pad. Instead, hand-wash them and hang them outside to dry.
1. Use a damp cloth with soapy water for spot cleaning.
2. Dry the pad by rubbing it with a towel.
Rug Gripper Cleaning Tips
Make sure rug grippers do not go into the washing machine. If the grippers lose their stickiness, simply rinse them with water and place them outside to dry before reusing them.
