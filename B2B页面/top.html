About FlexiSpot B2B Program
FlexiSpot, with facilities established globally across the USA, Europe, and Asia, has become a trusted brand for ergonomic furniture solutions. We collaborate with businesses worldwide to enhance living spaces with quality, comfort, and design innovation.
Contract Program: FlexiSpot focuses on delivering top-tier, ergonomic furniture tailored to meet the needs of businesses, institutions, and organizations. Our contract-grade products, crafted with durability and style in mind, come with exclusive service options to seamlessly integrate into various living environments.
Distribution Program: FlexiSpot supports our partners with competitive pricing, ready-to-ship inventory, and a reliable delivery network to meet orders of any scale. We also offer batch customization directly from our global facilities to accommodate your specific needs, ensuring high-quality and efficient fulfillment for large orders.
