What to consider when choosing a luggage?
Start by evaluating your travel requirements to determine the appropriate suitcase sizes size. When selecting your suitcase, consider the duration of your trip and the type of clothing you'll need. For short getaways, a smaller suitcase might suffice, allowing you to pack efficiently. However, for longer journeys, you'll likely need a larger suitcase to accommodate extra outfits, shoes, and personal items.
